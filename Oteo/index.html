WHO IS UNFUNGIBLE FOR?

Our solutions are not for pump and dump founders who are looking for a quick buck. Unfungible is for visionary founders and brands who are serious about building their longterm presence and influence in web3. We work with teams who have the following problems:
You Have No Time

You're busy building your business, refining your product, and leading your team. You have no time to execute on new marketing initiatives.

Lack Of A Proper Strategy

You're busy building your business, refining your product, and leading your team. You have no time to execute on new marketing initiatives. You're confident in your product, but aren't sure how to put it in front of the right people. You may have tried a couple marketing strategies, only to have the results disappoint.

You Don't Have Connections

The reality of the web3 space is that a lot of it comes down to who you know. Building the right relationships with the right people can be tricky and intimidating, but it's necessary.

You Don't Know What You Don't Know

You're navigating the web3 marketing landscape for the first time, and aren't sure what metrics to keep an eye on to assess your progress.

Inconsistent Growth

You're gaining traction on X & Discord, but month-over-month your growth is a guessing game. You haven't nailed down the strategy that'll help you build your community sustainably.

Lack Of Accountability

Being a Web3 founder can be a lonely journey and it's easy to fall off the wagon. You're looking for an expert team that'll keep you accountable.
